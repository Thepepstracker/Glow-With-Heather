GLP-1 Agonists (Overview)

GLP-1 agonists are a class of prescription medications used in metabolic and weight management, prescribed and supervised by licensed clinicians. This page is a plain-English educational overview only — it contains no dosing and is not medical advice.

What it is

GLP-1 receptor agonists are prescription medications that mimic a natural hormone involved in appetite and blood-sugar regulation.

Category & status

Prescription medication. GLP-1 agonists are prescription drugs. They must be prescribed and monitored by a licensed healthcare provider. We provide no dosing or usage instructions.

What the research explores

These medications are studied and used clinically for metabolic and weight-related conditions. Decisions about them belong between a patient and their clinician.
