MOTS-c

MOTS-c is a mitochondrial-derived peptide studied for its role in metabolism and cellular energy. It is a research compound in the human context — not an approved medication — and is presented here for education only, with no dosing guidance.

What it is

MOTS-c is a small peptide encoded within mitochondrial DNA, studied in metabolic research.

Category & status

What the research explores

Research explores MOTS-c in relation to metabolism, mitochondrial function, and energy biology, largely in lab and animal models.
