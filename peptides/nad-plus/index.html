NAD+

NAD+ (nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide) is a coenzyme found in every living cell, central to energy metabolism. It’s widely discussed in the longevity space and available in various consumer supplement forms; here we cover the science in plain English.

What it is

NAD+ is a naturally occurring coenzyme involved in cellular energy production and many metabolic reactions.

Category & status

Naturally occurring coenzyme (also sold as dietary-supplement precursors). NAD+ precursors are sold as dietary supplements; NAD+ itself is also used in clinical settings. We provide education, not dosing or medical advice.

What the research explores

Research explores NAD+ and its precursors in the context of cellular energy, metabolism, and aging biology. This is general education only.
