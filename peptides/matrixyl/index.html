Matrixyl

Matrixyl is a well-known family of peptide cosmetic ingredients used in topical anti-aging-style skincare. As a cosmetic active it’s associated with supporting the look of plumper, smoother skin in serums and moisturizers.

What it is

Matrixyl refers to peptide actives (such as palmitoyl pentapeptides) used as cosmetic ingredients in topical skincare.

Category & status

Cosmetic ingredient. Topical cosmetic actives found in many serums and creams. Not a drug.

What the research explores

Cosmetic studies have explored these peptides in relation to the visible signs of aging and skin appearance. General education only.
