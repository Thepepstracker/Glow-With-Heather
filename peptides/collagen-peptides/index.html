Collagen Peptides

Collagen peptides are hydrolyzed collagen sold widely as a dietary supplement powder. Popular in beauty and wellness routines, they’re among the most accessible peptide products — and one of Heather’s Amazon favorites for everyday glow support.

What it is

Collagen peptides are short chains of amino acids made by breaking down collagen protein, commonly sold as a flavorless powder.

Category & status

Dietary supplement. Collagen peptides are sold as food/dietary supplements. Not intended to diagnose, treat, or cure any condition.

What the research explores

Research has explored dietary collagen peptides in relation to skin appearance and general wellness. Shared here for education only.
