FAQ

How does it work?

Concentration = vial \u00F7 water. Draw volume = dose \u00F7 concentration. Units = draw volume \u00D7 100 on a U-100 syringe.

What is bacteriostatic water?

Sterile water with a small amount of benzyl alcohol to limit bacterial growth. The volume you add sets the final concentration.
